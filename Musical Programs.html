P R O G R A M S

She performed Natyasangeet in the programs like Lalana Devata Jagachi, 1960's Natyasangeet, MarmaBandhatali Thev(by Kaladwayee). Her songs from programs like Majhi Sangeet Sadhana, Ghananeela Ladiwala, and Manik Moti are very popular. She performed Gujrati songs, Garba, Helo at Mumbai, Zambia, London, America along with singer-actor Mohan Dandekar(Husband). She has given no. of musical performances and performed Theme-based programs on Doordarshan.
