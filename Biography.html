B I O G R A P H Y

Smt. Madhuvanti Dandekar, born on 23 March, 1948 in Pune, Maharashtra. Her maiden name is Mangala Laxman Sahasrabudhe.

At the age of 8, Madhuvanti Dandekar started learning classical Music at BHARAT GAYAN SAMAJ, Pune. She graduated (Sangeet Visharad) in Music when she was 15. In her school and colleges, there was her active participation in singing competitons, Music Dramas, Sanskrit speech competitions and she recieved so many prizes for the same. Later on, she married to Mohan Jagannath Dandekar who was the Professional Singer and Actor. He worked predominantly in Marathi Musical Dramas.After marriage, they both started working in Musical Dramas together.

Madhuvanti and Mohan Dandekar

They have started their own organization KALAMANJIRI. The moto behind starting this organisation is to promote traditional Marathi Music Daramas. They have performed well-known traditional Dramas like Saubhadra, Maanaapmaan, Vidyaharan SanshayKallol, Swayamvar and so many Musical Programs as well.

Madhuvanti and Mohan Dandekar

She learnt NATYASANGEET from the stalwarts of Music Dramas like Swarraj Chota Gandharva, SangeetBhushan Pt. Ram Marathe and Padmashri Jaymala Shiledar. Especially, she learnt Classical music, Natyasangeet, devotional songs and light classical from Swararaj Chota Gandharva. Her maximum output and best work is in the Marathi Music Dramas. She has learnt classical from Pt. Achyut Keshav Abhyankar (A.K.Abhyankar), Pt. Yashwantbua Joshi. She has also learnt Hindi and Urdu Gazals from Guru Vijay Chauhan(Gwalior). Her spiritual Guru is Late. Vimalaji Thakar.

She first made her mark with the beautiful rendition of Ruturaj Aaj Vani Aala (from H.M.V company) from Sangeet Madanachi Manjiri. which was written by legendary author Vidyadhar Gokhale. This was her very first professional Drama in which she played the main role of Manjiri and and later on the role of Leelavati (Leeladhar).

Smt. Madhuvanti Dandekar has contributed immeasurably to the field of Marathi Music Drama as a Singer and Actress. She worked in almost every popular Drama like Suvarnatula, Mandarmala, Swayamvar, Mrucchakatik ,Ekach Pyala, Saubhadra, SanshayKallol, Vidyaharan, Ranadundubhi, Bebandashahi, Jhala Mahar Pandharinath, Bhakta Pundalik, Pati gele ga Kathewadi, Dev Deenaghari Dhavala, Bhaktaraj Dhruvacha Taara(Chamakla Dhruvacha Taara), Krushnarjunyuddha, GarvaNirvaan, Hirva Chuda, Bhavbandhan, for around 55 years. She acted in Gujrati Dramas like Trajave Tolaya Bhagwan and Urdu Drama Nek Parveen.
A year ago, she recreated and directed Sangeet Madanachi Manjiri - A popular Music Drama.

Some acted in Music Dramas organised by many of the popular organisations like NatyaSuvarna, KalaSahakari, Sahitya Sangha NatyaShakha, Durvanchi Judi, JayMangal, RangaSharada Pratishthan, LalitKalaDarsha, KalaManjiri, Sahakari Manoranjan Mandal, NatyaParishad(Mumbai, Pune, Thane), Bharat Natya Mandir Pune, Varad Rangabhumi Pune, Goa Akadami, Chota Gandharva Concerns, Maharashtra Shasan(Urdu).

She acted with many popular artists like Chota Gandharva, Ram Marathe, NanaSaheb Phatak, Minakshibai, Prasad Savkar, Ramdas Kamat, Bhargavram Achrekar, Vishvanath Bagul, Bhalchandra Pendharkar, Bal Kolhatkar, Madhusudan Kolhatkar, Master Damle, Master Dattaram, Chittaranjan Kolhatkar, Narayan Bodas, Sharad Talwalkar, Daji Bhatawdekar, Ashok Saraf, Mama Pendse, Mai Bhide, Kanhopatrabai, Chandu Degvekar, Shri Joglekar, Rajani Joshi, Shripad Nevrekar, Chandu Parkhi, Prashant Damle, Nilu Phule, Ananta Jog, Jayshree Shejwadkar, Nanasaheb Shirgopikar, Jayant Savarkar, Suhas Bhalekar, Raja Gosavi, Ravi Patwardhan, Ramesh Bhatkar, Raja Mayekar, Ashwini Bhave, Suhas Joshi, Vamanrao Patwardhan, Kumar Dighe, Arvind Pilgaonkar, Shrikant Dadarkar, Shubhada Dadarkar, Vijay Gokhale, Dnyanesh Pendharkar, Shridhar Chafekar, Smita Talwalkar, Madhav Khadilkar, Suhasini Mulgaonkar, Vidyadhar Gokhale.

She delievered speech on Sangeet Natak and NatyaSangeet in a seminar organised by U.G.C.
She acted in a Marathi Movie Shapath Tula Balachi and also in the T.V serials like Athanga and Gharkul.

She teaches Natyasangeet to diploma students at Natyasangeet school run by of Mrs. Shubhada Dadarkar and Mr. Shrikant Dadarkar. She runs her own Music classes as well to teach Naatyasangeet, Classical, devotinal songs to the youngsters.

She enjoyed the company of many good people from the field of music, literature, poetry and it played a great impact on her life. She contributed a lot for the programs of Akashwani, Pune. She performed classical, Natyasangeet, devotional songs at Akashwani Sangeet Sammelan. Moreover, she has given concerts Music Programs at Panji, Hyderabad, Vijayvada, Kadappa, Vishakapattan, Ratnagiri, Gwalior, Delhi, Sangli, Solapur Akashwani centres since 1967. She acted in Sangeet SuvarnaTula and Madanachi Manjiri and Mandarmaala for Mumbai Doordarshan. Also, she acted in KeshavVandana program based on Legendary Keshavrao Bhosale.
