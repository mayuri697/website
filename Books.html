B O O K S

Smt.Madhuvanti Dandekar has written so many articles through FaceBook in the year 2020. The articles are fully about MARATHI NATYASANGEET. It contains the meaning of every single line of various Natyapadas. In 2022, she published her first book which contains all the articles she wrote. The name of the book is MADHUGANDHA NATYAGEETANCHA. The moto behind writing the articles and publishing the book is to bow the seeds of Marathi Music Dramas and Natyapadas in the young generation to make their music-learning journey more efficient through the correct use of words while singing.

If you want to know more, then you must read this book.

Moreover, she has translated and published 2 different books based on the Hindi devotional speeches of spiritual master Late Vimalaji Thakar. The translation of these speeches is in the Marathi Language. The name of the books are SATYACHI UPALABDHI and SANJIVAK SHAKTICHE VAHAK BANAVE.

Marathi Translations of the books of Late. Vimalaji Thakar.

She has written various articles from popular newspapers like Ma.Ta. Loksatta, Sakal, Pudhari. Some names of the articles are Vidyadhar Gokhale, Jyotsna Bhole, Manik Varma, Ram Marathe, Chota Gandharva, Kusum Apte, Swami Vivekananda, Yogi Shri Arvind, Jeevanyogini Vimalaji Thakar.
She wrote an article Namana Natawara in Kesari newspaper. The article contains brief information about Sangeet Natak writers, Music Directors, Artists of Sangeet Natak and Musicians as well.

She has given Music lectures of BA, MA (Music) at LalitKala Centre (SPPU). She got appointment from S.N.D.T college, Pune for the Referee of Natyasangeet PHD Department.
